This project analyzes sales, profit, customer segments, product performance, and regional trends using Power BI. 
The dashboard helps identify top-performing products, profitable regions, and overall business performance through interactive visualizations.

Page 1 provides an overview of business performance including total sales, profit, quantity sold, and order count. 
It also shows sales distribution by category, region, and monthly trends.

Page 2 focuses on product performance, highlighting the top-selling and most profitable products, category-wise profit, and detailed product information.

Page 3 analyzes customer and regional insights, showing sales by customer segment, ship mode, state-wise sales, and profit distribution across regions.
